ABOUT

Kingdom Hearts is a videogame series created in collaboration between Square Enix and Disney. The series is filled with various characters from each universe making appearances over the years since its release. The game series started in 2002 in Japan, introducing its original characters that tied with the characters from both Disney and Square Enix. At the moment the series consists of thirteen games titles that were released for multiple different platforms over the years. Still, since then these games have been made into a collection of separate games making it possible to experience all of the stories in a fluid motion on the same platform (Playstation). The game is well known for its complex story and music over the years and during its long journey its fans have been through a world filled with emotions, action, and friendship

MAIN STORY

KINGDOM HEARTS follows the main protagonist Sora, a Keyblade wielder, as he travels to many Disney worlds with Donald and Goofy to stop the Heartless invasion by sealing each world's keyhole and restoring peace to the realms. The Story focuses on the counterbalance of Light and Darkness and their existence. In the game, Kingdom hearts is a place where the hearts of the people who die go to.
